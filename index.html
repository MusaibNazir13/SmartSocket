Auto Turn ON/OFF Socket, once the battery of the device is fully charged the socket turns off automatically, and can be turned ON and OFF using web dashboard as well. Prevents battery degradation

*Note: By Prevention I mean slows down aging by 4x. If the battery usually degrades in 1 year, by our smartsocket it will degrade in 4 years.

Prevention of Li-Ion
Battery Degradation

Challenges we are addressing:
  • Overcharging – keeps Battery at high voltage constantly, stressing internal chemicals and reduces capacity of battery.
  • Heat Buildup – continuous charging generates heat, and heat accelerates battery aging by damaging cells.
  • Staying at 100% for long time – Holding battery charge for long time stresses battery chemistry, causing long term degradation

Proposed Solution &
System Architecture

Our IoT-Based Approach

We are trying to automate the power supply, by just controlling the supply going to the device, by getting battery status inputs from the device. Or by notification triggering,

In E-Vs:

The EVs are connected to smartphones, the smartphones are already getting the notifications from the vehicles once they are fully charged. By that notification we trigger the ESP82 endpoint to turn off the socket connected to relay module.

In Smartphones:

Smartphones nowadays come up with inbuilt routine/modes feature with different names. We can create a routine once the battery is charged until certain limit or 100%, trigger the endpoint of ESP82.
